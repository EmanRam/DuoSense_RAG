Q1-Q3 Revenue Report — FiscalYear 2024

Executive Summary:
Total revenue for the first three quarters reached $4.2 million, representing a 23% increase compared to the same period last year.

Revenue: $1.1 million
Key driver: Launch of the new subscription tier added 1,200 new customers.
Churn rate remained low at 3.2%.

Q2 Performance (April - June):
Revenue: $1.4 million
Key driver: Marketing campaign in Europe generated $380,000 in new contracts.

Q3 Performance (July - September):
